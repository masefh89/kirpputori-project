About US

Welcome to our Kirpputori! We are your friendly online marketplace for buying and selling second-hand items. From clothes and accessories to furniture and electronics, our platform makes it easy to find great deals and give items a second life.

Our mission is to promote sustainability, reduce waste, and create a community where everyone can find something unique. Join us today and discover how simple and fun thrifting can be!
