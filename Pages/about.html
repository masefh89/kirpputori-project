About Kirpputori

Welcome to Kirpputori! We are your local online marketplace for buying and selling second-hand items. Our mission is to create a sustainable community where everyone can find great deals and give items a second life.

Our Story

Kirpputori started as a small initiative to make thrifting easy and fun. We noticed that many useful items were going to waste, so we decided to provide a simple platform where people could list their items and connect with buyers in their community.

Why Choose Us?

  • Easy-to-use platform with a clean interface.
  • Safe and secure transactions.
  • Support for sustainability and second-hand culture.
  • A community-focused marketplace where everyone benefits.
